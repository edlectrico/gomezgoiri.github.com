Biography

Aitor Gómez Goiri is a Research Assistant at the MORElab research group who has also worked as an Assistant Professor at the University of Deusto. He finished a double-degree on Computer Engineering and Engineering in Industrial Organization in 2008 and a MSc in Software Development in 2010 at the University of Deusto. Currently he is a PhD student.
